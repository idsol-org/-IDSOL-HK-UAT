The Frontier of Data Innovation

Supporting Hong Kong's vision of being a global Data Science hub

Mission

As Hong Kong sets its sights on becoming a data science center of excellence, we focus on developing future leaders in this dynamic field.

The Global Stage for Data Science Prowess

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) is more than just a competition; it’s a global platform where the brightest minds in data science converge to showcase their skills and creativity. From predictive modeling to deep learning, IDSOL challenges participants to push the boundaries of what’s possible with data.

A Journey of Discovery and Achievement

The International Data Science Olympiad is more than just a competition—it’s a journey of discovery and achievement. As you delve into datasets and tackle challenging problems, you’ll uncover new insights, develop your skills, and push the boundaries of what you thought was possible.

A Showcase of Global Talent and Innovation

The International Data Science Olympiad is a showcase of global talent and innovation. It brings together participants from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, all united by a passion for data science. At IDSOL, you’ll have the opportunity to compete with and learn from some of the most talented data scientists in the world.

NOTICE

The International Data Science Olympiad ("IDSOL") would like to address the spread of misinformation regarding IDSOL and IBCOL in Hong Kong SAR China. Please be cautious of individuals or organisations claiming to represent IDSOL without proper authorisation or documentation. This extends to unsubstantiated allegations against persons affiliated with IDSOL in Hong Kong SAR, China.

All participants of IDSOL are reminded that countries and territories with official organising committees will have the necessary documentation to authorise their representation. Participants must adhere to the processes set forth by the official committees to compete and qualify.

If you are unsure whether a website or communication you have received is legitimate, please contact us.
